[SLIDE_START]
[TITLE_START]Introduction to Artificial Intelligence[TITLE_END]
Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines.
It enables systems to perform tasks that normally require human reasoning.
These tasks include learning, problem-solving, perception, and decision-making.
AI is widely used in modern applications such as search engines and virtual assistants.
[SLIDE_END]

[SLIDE_START]
[TITLE_START]Types of Artificial Intelligence[TITLE_END]
Narrow AI is designed to perform a specific task efficiently.
General AI aims to replicate full human cognitive abilities.
Super AI refers to intelligence surpassing human capabilities.
Most current real-world systems operate under the Narrow AI category.
[SLIDE_END]

[SLIDE_START]
[TITLE_START]Machine Learning and Its Importance[TITLE_END]
Machine Learning is a subset of AI focused on learning from data.
It allows systems to improve performance without explicit programming.
Common techniques include supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning.
Machine Learning powers recommendation systems, fraud detection, and predictive analytics.
[SLIDE_END]

[SLIDE_START]
[TITLE_START]Future and Impact of AI[TITLE_END]
AI is transforming industries such as healthcare, finance, and transportation.
It helps automate repetitive tasks and improves efficiency.
Ethical considerations like fairness, transparency, and safety are important.
The future of AI will involve deeper integration into everyday human life.
[SLIDE_END]
